1. Grade Checker
Take a score as input and print the grade based on the following:
90+ : "A"
80-89 : "B"
70-79 : "C"
60-69 : "D"
Below 60 : "F"
here we used a basic if else statement to carry out marks and all.
Code:
x = int(input("Please enter your score: "))
if x>=90:
    print("A")
elif x>80:
    print("B")
elif x>70:
    print("C")
elif x>=60:
    print("D")
elif x<60:
    print("F")
